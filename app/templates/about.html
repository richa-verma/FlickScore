About Us

FlickScore is a research site run by SALSA Lab at IIIT Delhi. FlickScore aims to use "collaborative filtering" technology to make recommendations of movies that you might enjoy, and to help you avoid the ones that you won't. Based on the movie ratings you provide, FlickScore's objective is to generate personalized predictions for movies you haven't seen yet. FlickScore is put in place to create a unique research vehicle in the form of a dataset for interested undergraduates and graduate students researching various aspects of personalization and filtering technologies.
